Masala Tea

Recipe Description

Masala chai is a popular beverage throughout South Asia, originating in the early modern Indian subcontinent.

Chai(Tea) is often prepared unspiced in most household but adding herbs and spices gives it a exciting flavour that is hard to forget. Perfect for special ocassions or when you have guest in your house.

Ingredients

  • 1 Â½ cups water
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, chopped
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 5 pods green cardamom pods, crushed and cinnamon sticks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespooons white sugar

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Add ginger, cloves, cinnamon stick, green cardamom, and black cardamom; simmer until flavors infuse, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add tea bags; simmer for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk and sugar; bring back to a boil. Strain tea into mugs.
