Lemonade

Description

This is one of those drinks that don't need no explation. It is widely popular. Nothing like a nice cold glass of lemonade in hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • cold Water, ice cubes
  • 1 fresh lemon
  • 3 tablespoons Sugar
  • Soda(optional)

Steps

  1. Squeeze lemon in a glass filled with water and ice cubes.
  2. Add sugar and soda and stir well.
