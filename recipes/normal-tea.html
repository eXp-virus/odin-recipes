Simple tea recipe

Description

This tea bangs yo. Trust me. So simple yet so effective.

Ingredients

  • Half cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • Tea bags or tea leaves
  • Sugar, small ginger, 2 cinnamon

Steps

  1. Bring half cup of water to boil.
  2. Add crushed ginger and cinnamon.
  3. Add tea bags or tea leaves and let it boil for 1 minute.
  4. Finally add milk and bring it to boil 2-3 times.
